Jason M. Munro

Postdoctoral Research Scientist,
Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory

Hello! I am a scientist working at the intersection of materials science, informatics, and high-performance computing. Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher in the group of Kristin Persson at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory.

Much of my research focuses on leveraging physical symmetries and informatics based tools to help in the computational design and discovery of new materials. I am also actively involved in the development of the Materials Project.

Listed here are software packages I have contributed to and articles I have published as part of my research. For more details about my work, please see my full cv.
